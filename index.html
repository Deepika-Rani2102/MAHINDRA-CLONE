FAQS

Established in 1945 by JC and KC Mahindra and Ghulam Mohammed, Mahindra & Mohammed began as a steel trading firm and later ventured into manufacturing and selling larger MUVs. It was renamed Mahindra & Mahindra in 1947. Today, Mahindra Automotive is one of India's leading manufacturers of passenger and commercial vehicles.
Mahindra and Mahindra, a prominent automotive company has a widespread global presence in regions like APAC, Africa & Middle East, and South Central America. Its footprint spans across Australia, Bangladesh, Bhutan, Indonesia, New Zealand, Nepal, Sri Lanka, UAE, Kenya, Mozambique, Morocco, South Africa, Tunisia, Chile, Guatemala, and Peru.
Mahindra Automotive offers an extensive lineup of Mahindra vehicles which include SUVs, electric SUVs, Pik-ups, Electric Vehicles, Trucks, Small Commercial Vehicles, and buses. Its range includes various Mahindra cars, including new models, catering to a broad spectrum of customers.
Mahindra is India’s no. 1 electric 3 wheeler manufacturer company with the flagship Treo. The all – electric XUV 400, a fun-to-drive C – Segment Electric SUV is the latest addition with its bold Born Electric Vision, Mahindra is set to reimagine the world of electric SUVs. Visit https://mahindraelectricautomobile.com/ to see the line of futuristic SUV concepts that are set to release soon.
